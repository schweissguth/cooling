3.5kW DX Split

The Uniflair RM 3.5kW is a DX split cooling system.  In additional to all the Uniflair RM cooling benefits, the DX split keeps U-space consumption small by splitting it up into internal and external pieces – which means this system is designed to be room neutral i.e. the heat pulled from the rack is not rejected into the IT space.  This keeps the rack temperature consistent and stable.

  • Benefits
    • Cools virtually any rack up to 3.5kW
      • enough cooling to cool most single racks
      • no need for overprovisioned room based cooling (reduced cost, complexity, etc)
      • can also be used to introduce stability in environments with existing, unstable cooling
    • Only 5U height
      • fits discretely inside rack therefore no need to deploy external cooling
      • Higher cooling output in a smaller size because positioning is closest to the source; also greatly improves predictability
      • room neutral so therefore integration-friendly and closely positioned to IT equip for maximum predictability and smallest form factor >>can also serve as primary cooling in a room with
      • commercial cooling to prevent heat build up when building HVAC turns off for the weekend.
    • Bottom mounted
      • placed in bottom of rack to ensure maximum available and accessible U space
      • discretely placed to maximize integration potential
      • easily positioned into place and transportable with rack
    • Room-neutral (DX split)
      • the DX split system is designed to be room-neutral, which means the heat is fully rejected to the outdoors and therefore entirely self-sufficient (i.e. not self-contained) >>this does require
      • however mechanical piping between the indoor and outdoor pcs
    • Flexible for customer "self-contained“ configuration
      • to simplify installation, the condensing unit can be installed on top of the rack or adjacent to the rack so that the system essentially becomes self-contained. This way the system provides
      • point-cooling inside the rack while rejecting the heat outside of it. For this to work, the room in which the rack is placed must be large enough to absorb the heat or there must be sufficient
      • ventilation to manage it.

  • Key Features
    • 3.5kW rated cooling capacity
      • enough cooling to cool most single racks
      • no need for overprovisioned room based cooling (reduced cost, complexity, etc)
      • can also be used to introduce stability in environments with existing, unstable cooling
    • 5U rack mounted
      • fits discretely inside rack therefore no need to deploy external cooling
      • Higher cooling output in a smaller size because positioning is closest to the source; also greatly improves predictability
      • room neutral so therefore integration-friendly and closely positioned to IT equip for maximum predictability and smallest form factor
      • can also serve as primary cooling in a room with commercial cooling to prevent heat build up when building HVAC turns off for the weekend.
      • Bottom mounted
        - placed in bottom of rack to ensure maximum available and accessible U space
        - discretely placed to maximize integration potential
      • easily positioned into place and transportable with rack
    • 750 m3/h rated airflow
      • High velocity airflow is designed to maximize cooling capacity in the smallest possible footprint while the high sensible cooling coil prevents the risk of moisture blow off into rack
      • Airflow and cooling capacity is designed to match server CFM/kW requirements unlike conventional comfort HVAC systems.
    • Stacking kit (ETL version only, optional)
      • Ability to insert duplicate cooling units inside one rack to increase cooling output or redundancy by removing the air plenum to allow air passage from unit below.
    • Robust design
      • Designed for mission critical application to continuously modulate cooling output 24x7 unlike conventional commercial HVAC systems with static on/off control.
      • continous modulation reduces the number of on/off cycles thus preserving the equipment life
    • 20m max equiv distance +/-5m max lift
      • Piping distance and lift allows the external unit to be strategically placed
      • for simpler installation, the condensing unit (outdoor unit) can be positioned near the rack whereas to pull the heat from in the rack and reject it outside. Thus not requiring extensive pipe routing through walls, etc. However this requires:
         - the rack to be air tight (sealed)
         - there to be either an existing cooling system for the room or a very large plenum (such as an industrial plant floor)
    • Condensing unit design
      • Compact design minimizes floorspace consumption, flexible mounting options
      • removes compressor from IT space and therefore allows easy serviceability of all critical components outside the data center minimizing IT space interruption
      • allows indoor unit to be placed on UPS due to low power draw
    • R-410a precharged
      • non-precharged for Europe is also an option
    • Electronic expansion valve
      • High accuracy and quick response time ensures cooling operation outputs constant cold air temp event when IT conditions change
    • Heat assist
      • Ensures stable cooling operation even under small IT loads
    • -40 degree winter kit (optional)
      • Reduces the required minimum start up outdoor temperature of the system to support extreme climates as low as -40C
    • Inverter rotary compressor
      • keeps constant cold air temp inside the rack Automatically adjusts to changes in IT load, no user config necessary
      • Soft start
        • Reduces inrush current to reduce breaker sizing
        • allows rack mounted unit to be powered via UPS
    • Variable speed fans
      • Continous and automatic modulation of fan speed to match airflow requirements of IT gear at any given time
    • Upward air plenum
      • Reduces inrush current to reduce breaker sizing
      • allows rack mounted unit to be powered via UPS
    • Top or bottom connections
      • Various pipe kit accessories to adapt according to the layout of the IT space and improve installation experience
    • Coil guard
      • Prevents damage to do wind, debris, and protection against accidently mishandling.
    • Condensate pump (optional)
      • Removes moisture collected from the air by pumping it away and out of the IT space to ensure no risk to IT gear.
    • User Interface (optional)
      • Not required for operation but enables local control for viewing and customizing operational setpoints and status checking.
      • unit can be start up and operate without it and run at default settings to keep installation simple and straightforward and cost effective
    • BMS
      • Ability to connect to the existing BMS or provide a serial connection for external control
    • Network card (roadmap)
      • Q3-Y2020 is target date for this. will include bacnet
    • Installation (piping) kits
      • Various pipe kit choices for above rack, under rack, and under raised floor to accelerate installation time and easiness
      • Pipe kits are routed through side channels of rack and if necessary can be conformed to fit.
    • Crankcase heater
      • retains oil lubrication to prevent damage to the bearings and other wearable parts
      • also prevents possible damage caused by pockets of non-compressible air
    • Handles
      • easy to install, single person can lift
