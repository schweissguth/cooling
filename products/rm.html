Uniflair RM

 
  • Value Proposition
    • The easiest way to integrate cooling into your micro data center
  • Benefits
    • Right sized for edge micro data centers
      • Designed to fit into virtually any IT rack
      • perfect for both new or retrofit
      • Small size (only 5U height)
      • No need for overprovisioned room based cooling (reduced cost, complexity, etc)
    • Built for mission critical operation
      • Designed for 24x7 continuous operation
      • Easy serviceability of critical components outside the IT space
      • Supports extreme climates as low as -40C
    • Closely positioned to IT gear
      • Always ensures rack temperature stays constant
        • Precise ±1ºC temperature control
      • Infinitely adjustable capacity modulation between 30-100%
        • automatically adjusts cooling output according to changes in IT load
      • Improved airflow predictability
    • Integration ready
      • Easily positioned into place and transportable with rack
      • Ensures maximum available U space for the user
      • Ability to connect to MODBUS serial connection
  • Applications
    • Existing small server rooms / network closets
      • rooms with no cooling or cooling from building HVAC that does not operate daily
      • rooms with excessive, expensive, over provisioned, and/or inefficient room cooling
      • for use in any new or existing rack with bottom 5U available
    • New micro data centers
      • for use in standard IT rack (such as AR3300)
      • for use in semi-sealed IT rack such as AR3100 with front and rear RACS
      • for use in fully-sealed IT rack such as custom solution, Climasys, SmartBunker, or 3rd party system integrator
      • customers who want to bay two racks together with RACS and share cooling between
      • for use in any new or existing rack with bottom 5U available
    • Hot spots
      • customers who are having troubles with hot spots at the rack
      • room or ceiling hung units that, by nature, allow hotspots because they aren't positioned closely to the IT.
      • Supplement unpredictable building HVAC
      • customers looking to replace competitors' non-mission critical ceiling mount units
      • replacing any unit in the space that is not mission critical (LG, Mitsubishi, Daiken, existing commercial HVAC, etc)
      • for added redundancy, or as primary cooling with existing cooling as backup
    • Integrators
      • to pre-package or assemble-on-site the total micro data center
      • rack cooling piece can be pre-installed in rack
  • SKUs
    • 3.5kW DX Split (ACRMD4K series)
    • 3.5kW SC (ACRMS4K series) (roadmap)
    • 5kW DX Split (ACRMD5K) (roadmap)
  • Key Features
    • Powerful capacity
      • enough cooling to cool most single racks
      • no need for overprovisioned room based cooling (reduced cost, complexity, etc)
      • can also be used to introduce stability in environments with existing, unstable cooling
    • Inverter compressor
      • keeps constant cold air temp inside the rack
      • Automatically adjusts to changes in IT load, no user config necessary
    • Variable speed fans
      • Continous and automatic modulation of fan speed to match airflow requirements of IT gear at any given time
    • Condensate pump (optional)
      • Removes moisture collected from the air by pumping it away and out of the IT space to ensure no risk to IT gear.
      • in future a baking-tray could be developed to “evaporate” moisture collected and drained outside the rack so that no condensate piping or drain is required.
    • BMS
      • Ability to connect to the existing BMS or provide a serial connection for external control
    • Network card (roadmap)
      • BACNET
      • MODBUS TCP/IP
      • EcoStruxure
